



# twisted_twine_workshoppe_app
An app for an individual who is looking to have all (primarily) knitting and crochet commission needs in one place.

<img src = "https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2e832282-d6ef-4d04-94a5-62e2c176581d" width="300" height="600">

(NB: the homepage was designed with my personal sensibilties in mind)

Features and their current status include
- Home Page containing:
  - The number of pending commissions (completed)
  - The number of commission currently being worked on (completed)
  - Total income |from commissions| (completed)
  - Total expense |from commissions| (completed)
  - In |your personal capital that you've used for commissions| (completed)
  - Out |income you've taken for yourself| (completed)
  - Wallet |money from income and personal capital that is left after expense and personal income is taken| (completed) 

- Timer |Although this is called a timer, it is technically a stopwatch for how long you spend creating personal projects or commissions| (completed)
